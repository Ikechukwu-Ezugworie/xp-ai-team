# Design Simplicity & Coherence Rubric

Score each dimension 1–5.

| Score | Simplicity | Coherence |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| 5 | No unnecessary abstractions; every component earns its place | All parts use consistent patterns, naming, and structure |
| 4 | Minor redundancy; generally lean | Small inconsistencies that don't impede understanding |
| 3 | Some over-engineering or duplication | Mixed patterns; requires effort to follow |
| 2 | Notable bloat; unclear responsibilities | Incoherent structure; naming conflicts |
| 1 | Severely over-complex or tangled | No discernible design pattern |

**Total score = Simplicity + Coherence (max 10)**
